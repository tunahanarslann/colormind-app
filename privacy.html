By using ColorMind you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device data: OS version, device model, language.
  • Usage data: In-game progress (levels, scores) stored locally only.
  • Ad identifiers: Google Mobile Ads SDK may access the device's advertising ID for serving ads.

2. How We Use Data

  • Improve stability and gameplay experience.
  • Save progress and preferences on the device.
  • Display ads in compliance with platform policies.

3. Sharing

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Receives anonymized device/ad data to show ads.
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • No other third parties receive personal data.

4. Retention

  • Game progress is stored locally via AsyncStorage and removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Ad data retention follows Google's policies.

5. Children's Privacy

ColorMind targets users 4+. If you are under 4, please use the app under parental guidance.

6. Your Choices

  • Reset or limit the advertising ID in device settings.
  • Contact us at colormindspprt@gmail.com for privacy questions or requests.
